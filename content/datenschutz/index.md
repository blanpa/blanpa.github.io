## 1. Privacy at a Glance

### General Information

This privacy policy explains what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data that can be used to personally identify you.

### Data Collection on This Website

**Who is responsible for data collection on this website?**

Data processing on this website is carried out by the website operator. You can find their contact details in the [Legal Notice](/impressum/).

**How is your data collected?**

Your data is collected in part by you providing it — for example, through the contact form. Other data is collected automatically or with your consent when you visit the website by IT systems. This is primarily technical data (e.g., internet browser, operating system, time of page access).

## 3. Contact Form

When you use the contact form on this website, your information (name, email address, subject, message) is transmitted to the service **Formspree** (Formspree Inc., USA) and forwarded to me via email.

- **Purpose:** Processing your inquiry
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR (pre-contractual measures) or Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest)
- **Storage period:** Your data is processed by Formspree in accordance with their privacy policy. More information: [Formspree Privacy Policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/)

## 4. Your Rights

You have the right at any time to:
- **Access** your stored personal data
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** of your data
- **Restriction** of processing
- **Object** to processing
- **Data portability**

To exercise these rights, you can contact me at any time via email at **blanpa@proton.me**.

## 5. SSL Encryption

This website uses SSL encryption for security purposes. You can recognize an encrypted connection by the browser address bar changing from "http://" to "https://".
